About Divya Dyuti

We started our journey on 1st January 2017 with Two Musketeers...Me, Gopa Trivedi and Sanjoy Bhattacharya. Through uniqueness, creativity and authenticity 'Divya Dyuti will turn you special' is our motto. So starting from the beginning we are maintaining its uniqueness in both male and female attire. That means all the products of Divya Dyuti are exclusive and unique.

We value every customers' emotion and try to express our inner imagination through our creations so that every customer feels special and unique among many.
